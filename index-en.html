Welcome

Istrian Tranquility Between Vineyards and Olive Groves

Villa VinoSelo sits quietly amid the Istrian landscape near Vranje Selo, Vižinada – surrounded by vineyards, olive groves and old trees, under plenty of sun and blue sky. A charm reminiscent of Tuscany, just a 15-minute drive from the sea.

The property is divided into two completely independent, unconnected apartments, each with its own entrance: the larger main apartment on the upper floor and a smaller apartment on the garden floor – ideal for two families or groups who still value their privacy. It also features a private pool (8×4 m), an outdoor whirlpool, an infrared sauna and a summer kitchen with a stone grill in the spacious, fenced garden. Shops, restaurants and the village centre of Vižinada are just 1 km away.
